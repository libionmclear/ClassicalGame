## Rome

2. Julius Caesar — lean patrician in his 50s, receding hair, red paludamentum over gilded cuirass, laurel wreath, faint smile of a man who has already decided
3. Augustus — composed young emperor, toga with purple border over light armor, idealized ageless features, hand slightly raised in address
4. Cato the Elder — weathered stern farmer-senator in his 60s, plain wool toga, close-cropped grey hair, holding a scroll, disapproving mouth

6. Marcus Agrippa — broad practical soldier-engineer in his 40s, heavy brow, plain cuirass, bronze dividers in hand, harbor rope over shoulder
